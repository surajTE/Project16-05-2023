The power of strategic question at various stages of the design process is what I value most about design. My primary contribution to a design group is to guide and deepen the discussion by providing questions and aiding in the formulation of where and what to learn at various stages of the project. Because this strategy facilitates the investigation of new ideas and the promotion of new solutions and a deeper understanding, team members may approach challenges from a variety of perspectives. We may use strategic questioning to unlock the full potential of a design project and ensure that the end product meets or exceeds expectations.
